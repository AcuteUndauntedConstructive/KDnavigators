Global Career Opportunities Through KDnavigators

We proudly offer specialized training and direct placements into the world’s leading shipping companies. Here’s a detailed overview of the roles we prepare you for:

Fire Technology Officer

Learn advanced firefighting and emergency response skills onboard ships Certified training under DGT standards ensures you protect lives and vessels at sea and port

Fitter

Become an expert in ship structure fabrication, fitting, and repairs. Fitters play a critical role in maintaining a vessel's physical integrity and safety

Electrician

Work on shipboard electrical systems, handling wiring, installation, troubleshooting, and critical repairs for lighting, communication, and control systems

Deck Rating

Support cargo operations, ship navigation, and onboard safety Deck Ratings are essential team members ensuring smooth voyages

Engine Rating

Maintain and operate ship engines, generators, and auxiliary machinery in the engine room, ensuring optimal performance during sea voyages

General Purpose (G.P.) Rating

Dual-trained in both deck and engine departments, G.P. Ratings are versatile assets onboard, offering wider career opportunities across departments.

Electrical Officer (Trainee Electrical Officer / TEO Pathway)

Manage electrical systems, high-voltage equipment, navigation support devices, alarms, and power distribution on board merchant vessels.

Chief Engineer

After gaining years of sea experience and clearing competency exams, candidates can rise to the position of Chief Engineer — the highest technical officer on the ship. Responsible for the complete management of the engine room, technical operations, maintenance, and safety.
