Shipping & Marine Careers Your Gateway to a High-Paying Life at Sea

Deonarayan Kumar, Founder & Director 03 Feb 2025

Have you ever dreamt of a job that lets you travel the world, earn in lakhs, and build a highly respected career? The shipping and marine industry offers exactly that — especially for Indian engineers, diploma holders, and 10+2 students who are willing to take the leap. In this blog, we explore what a career in the shipping and marine sector looks like, what kind of roles are in demand, expected salaries, certifications required, and how KDnavigators can help you achieve global success.

Global Demand: With 90% of the world’s trade moving by sea, the demand for skilled marine professionals is only growing. High Salaries: Entry-level positions can start at ₹40,000–₹70,000/month, while officer-level roles can reach ₹2.5+ lakhs/month. Tax-Free Income: In many cases, earnings made while working outside Indian waters are tax-free under Indian law. Career Growth: You can rise from a trainee to Chief Engineer or even become a Marine Superintendent. Adventure & Prestige: Marine careers are challenging, adventurous, and highly respected.

Top Shipping Career Roles 1. Deck Rating Assists with navigation, cargo handling, and onboard safety Requires STCW, STSDSD, INDOS, and CDC 2. Engine Rating Works in the ship’s engine room and assists with machinery Great for mechanical/diploma holders 3. GP Rating (General Purpose) Dual-trained in deck and engine work Ideal for those who want flexibility 4. Electrical Officer / TEO (Trainee Electrical Officer) Manages ship’s electrical and navigation systems High demand + high salary role 5. Marine Fitter / Fabricator Works with ship structure, welding, and engine repairs Ideal for ITI and vocational students 6. Fire Technology Officer Trained in shipboard fire response and emergency handling Crucial for onboard safety and disaster management What Qualifications Are Required? 10+2 PCM or equivalent Diploma / ITI in Mechanical, Electrical, Fitter, or Marine fields B.Tech / BE in relevant streams Basic English & Physical Fitness per DG Shipping norms Mandatory Certifications for Marine Jobs Every shipping company requires internationally approved training, such as: STCW (PST, PSSR, FPFF, EFA) STSDSD (Security Training) INDOS Registration CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate) At KDnavigators, all certifications are DG Shipping-approved. "

Shipping and marine careers are not just jobs — they’re global gateways. With the right mindset, training, and career partner like KDnavigators, you can transform your life.

Why Start Your Shipping Career with KDnavigators? 19+ years of international shipping experience Govt.-approved certifications included in all programs Direct placement tie-ups with companies like MSC and Wallem Affordable, transparent pricing (₹16,400–₹21,700 packages) Post-placement career growth mentorship "With the right skills and support, you can go from a small town to sailing the world — and earning in lakhs.

Time to Get a Job After Training Most candidates are placed within 2–6 months after completing their training and documentation. Placement depends on your discipline, interview readiness, and performance in training.
